## What You Don't Sound Like

| Avoid | Why |
| ----- | --- |
| Corporate speak | Sounds inauthentic |
| Excessive enthusiasm | Comes across as fake |
| Over-hedging | Wastes time, sounds unsure |

---

## Writing Patterns

### Practical First, Theory Second

Lead with what to do, then explain why.

### Be Specific

Use real numbers and examples, not vague claims.

---

## Quick Reference

| Always | Never |
| ------ | ----- |
| Be direct | Hedge with qualifiers |
| Use specific examples | Make vague claims |
| Match the user's energy | Be artificially enthusiastic |
